Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline your IT experience (if any).

My interest in IT is growing gradually as it seems to be a very fun but challenging subject. There was no specific time, event or person that sparked my interest in IT but it was because I realised that IT is a very important necessity in the world nowadays and will provide job opportunities if I do well.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

Previously, I was studying at Monash University and it was quite a hectic time due to the homework load and the difficulty of assignments and exams. It was also a 1 hour and 15 minute train ride to university everyday. I decided to move to RMIT because of the difference in learning styles as RMIT is more chill and at a slower pace which would allow me to cope better rather than stressing hard about balancing my life, work and studies. I did not do very well at Monash University and kind of failed a few subjects, however at RMIT it has been a much smoother ride for me. I have passed every subject in my Bachelor of Business course and hope to pass and do well in all my IT subjects. :)

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I expect to learn how to interact with people efficiently as I believe this is an important ability in the IT field. More importantly, I would like to learn more about coding and programing because I think it would be cool to be able to design and create my own applications and websites.
