The Big Five Project Personality Test


 
The results for this test are completely opposite to the results from the Myer-Briggs Test. My thoughts on these results are that they are somewhat accurate, however I responded to a few questions with uncertainty so the results weren't too precise.

The results from personality tests usually have me reflecting on myself and whether or not the results I got were accurate or meaningful and helps with self-realisation. It helps me figure out whether I like to work with others, how I interact with others and their emotions, my thinking process etc.

According to these results, I tend to shy away from social situations which is true in some cases. These results might influence me in a way which will cause me to participate in more social situations as I don't want to be introverted.
As mentioned on the other page (The Myer-Briggs Test), from the results of these tests I will look at finding team members who are willing to be social and communicate effectively. As I am not very creative and can't really think out of the box, I will try to get help from people who are more effective in the creative department of things.
