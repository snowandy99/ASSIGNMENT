The Myer-Briggs Test



This is the result that I got from the Myer-Briggs Test. The test has provided a very accurate description of my characteristics as I believe that the characteristics mentioned in the results align with my characteristics (my own perspective on my characteristics). This has me believing that this test is somewhat reliable and possibly a good way to learn more about a person's personality

These results influence my behavior in a team as I like to socialise and discuss in a group rather than individual work. It will also influence my thinking process as I usually focus on the feelings of others rather than thinking using logic.

I will take these results into account when forming a group by looking for team members who like to discuss the task, their ideas and thoughts etc. I will also look out for team members who are flexible with time and don't have a fixed schedule for when things should be done.
