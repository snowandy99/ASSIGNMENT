The Creativity Test



 
The results to this test are quite accurate as I find myself being able to see things through many perspectives and connect information and people with one another. As you can see from the results,it seems that I lack the ability to force myself to push on to achieve my goal and that I am easily influenced by the feelings and thoughts of others.

These results will help me grasp the idea that I am somewhat lazy and not self-driven and that if I want to move on in life, I need to work hard. I should worry more about what's best for me and less about what people might think of me. Without understanding my weaknesses, I won't be able to improve and achieve much.

As a team member, these results will drive me to put more effort into the work I do rather than aiming for just a passing mark. When it comes to forming a team, I will try to find people who are willing to let me work at my own pace and will communicate effectively if they need something, I won't be able to work with people who are one-dimensional and can only see one answer for a situation,
