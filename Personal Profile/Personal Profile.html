Personal Profile

I have taken part in 3 different personality/characteristics test which are used by employers to learn more about their employees and/or future employees. This is important for employers as it is hard to recruit and build a team of incompatible workers or those who do not meet certain requirements.

The three tests I have done are:

  • The Myer-Briggs Test
  • The Big Five Personality Test
  • The Creativity Test
