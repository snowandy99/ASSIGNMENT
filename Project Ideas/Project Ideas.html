Project  Ideas

My project idea would be an application which is a combination of the smartphone application 'wickr' which is an encrypted messenger and 'Carousell' which is a website / smartphone app which allows people to buy and sell their new/used items by posting photos of their goods onto the app.


My application will be on smartphone and provide a service which will allow a safe transaction between buyers and sellers through encrypted messages to prevent the leakage of sensitive information and an escrow service in order to protect buyers from scams due to the risks of bank transfers.

Motivation for this project

My motivation for this project idea is due to the increasing usage of online shopping and with that, comes an increase in cyber crimes and fraud. Everyday there are cases of scamming where people will bank transfer 'sellers' and then not receiving the item they have purchased or where information such as credit card details and addresses have been leaked. My project idea will aim to help reduce the risk of being scammed and being 'doxxed' (the act of revealing someone's personal information on the internet)


Outcome

If this project was to be made successfully, it could prevent a lot of scams and fraudulent activity due to the ever increasing use of technology and online shopping. This app will provide a safe and secure platform for buyers and sellers to do what they need to do without being at risk of being exposed or stolen from.
